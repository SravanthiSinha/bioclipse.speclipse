About NMRShiftDB plugin

The NMRShiftDB plugin offers a connection of Bioclipse to the NMRShiftDB database. For information about NMRShiftdb, see here. The functions of the plugin are explained in the subsections of this document.

For scripting this plugin, see the general scripting guide and the Speclipse scripting functionality help.
